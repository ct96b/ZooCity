# Primary Political Factions by District

Each major district in ZooCity has its own political leadership and representative groups. These factions shape local policy, manage resources, and represent their residents in citywide decisions.

## Downtown
- City Council (Central government, meets in City Hall)
- Inari Cultural Committee

## Savanna Central
- Savanna Central Council
- Little Rodentia Neighborhood Board
- Gnu York Business Association

## Tundratown
- Tundratown Assembly
- Pride Ridge Elders

## Sahara Square
- Sahara Square Council
- Hyenahurst Community Leaders

## Rainforest District
- Rainforest District Council
- Vine Country Growers Cooperative

## Meadowlands
- Meadowlands Council
- Wooly Rocks Grazers Union

## Nocturnal Ranges
- Nocturnal Alliance
- Happytown Residents Association

## Outback Island
- Outback Rangers
