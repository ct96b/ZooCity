# Major Organizations of ZooCity

This file summarizes the key organizations, agencies, and associations that help run, protect, and enrich life in ZooCity. Each group is modeled after real-world institutions and can be used for story hooks, NPCs, or worldbuilding.

## Government & Civic
- ZPD: Zootopia Police Department
- ZFR: Zootopia Fire/Rescue
- ZSS: Zootopia Security Service (military/state security)
- ZPS: Zootopia Public Schools
- ZPRS: Zootopia Parks and Recreation Services
- ZTA: Zootopia Transit Authority
- ZHA: Zootopia Housing Authority
- ZEA: Zootopia Environmental Agency
- ZDA: Zootopia Development Agency
- ZCA: Zootopia Cultural Alliance

## Labor & Industry
- ZSW: Zootopia Steel Workers
- ZAW: Zootopia Auto Workers
- ZTU: Zootopia Teachers Union
- ZCU: Zootopia Construction Union
- ZFA: Zootopia Farmers Association

## Commerce & Business

## Social & Community

- ZYA: Zootopia Youth Association

## Health & Welfare

## Law & Justice

## Science & Education
- ZSA: Zootopia Science Academy
- ZLA: Zootopia Library Association


## Media
- ZNN: Zootopia News Network (mainstream, modeled after CNN)
- ZBC: Zootopia Broadcasting Corporation (public broadcaster, modeled after BBC/NPR)
- ZTV: Zootopia Television (entertainment and news, modeled after network TV)
- Z24: Zootopia 24-Hour News (sensationalist, modeled after cable news)
- ZIT: Zootopia Independent Tribune (independent, investigative journalism)
- ZPost: Zootopia Post (tabloid, rumors, and gossip)
- ZWeb: Zootopia Web News (online-only, blogs, and social media)

# Media Literacy Hooks
- Some outlets are reliable on certain topics but not others.
- Some may have hidden agendas, biases, or sensationalize stories.
- Encourage players to compare sources, check facts, and discuss who to trust and why.
