# Tundratown Gangs

Small-time gangs operating in Tundratown, often involved in smuggling, illegal gambling, and “cold” jobs. Sometimes work for Mr. Big, sometimes independent.

- **Activities:** Smuggling, gambling, theft, protection rackets.
- **Territory:** Tundratown, Frozen Jaw Bay, Icy Lake.
- **Notable Members:** Various gang leaders and crews.
- **Rivals:** Each other, Mr. Big’s Crime Family, ZPD.
