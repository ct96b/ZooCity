# Corrupt Officials and Businesses

Some criminal activity is hidden within legitimate businesses or involves bribery and corruption in local councils or guilds. These groups operate in the shadows, manipulating city politics and trade.

- **Activities:** Bribery, embezzlement, rigged contracts, money laundering.
- **Territory:** Citywide, especially in business and political circles.
- **Notable Members:** Corrupt council members, business owners, fixers.
- **Rivals:** Honest officials, ZPD, investigative journalists.
