# Weaselton’s Black Market

A loose network of smugglers, fences, and petty criminals led by Duke Weaselton. Specializes in stolen goods, knockoff products, and small-time cons.

- **Activities:** Smuggling, fencing, black market sales, cons.
- **Territory:** Savanna Central, Rainforest District, and back alleys citywide.
- **Notable Members:** Duke Weaselton, various small-time crooks.
- **Rivals:** Mr. Big’s Crime Family, ZPD.
