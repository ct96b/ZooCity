# Mr. Big’s Crime Family

Led by the arctic shrew Mr. Big, this organization is the most powerful and respected criminal syndicate in ZooCity. They control racketeering, protection, and “ice” (frozen goods) operations, especially in Tundratown and Downtown.

- **Activities:** Protection rackets, smuggling, black market trade, and “icing” enemies.
- **Territory:** Tundratown, Downtown, and parts of Savanna Central.
- **Notable Members:** Mr. Big, Koslov (bodyguard), family associates.
- **Rivals:** Street gangs, independent smugglers.
