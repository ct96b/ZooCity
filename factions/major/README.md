# Major Factions of ZooCity

ZooCity is home to a variety of influential groups, each shaping the city in unique ways. These major factions include government bodies, business coalitions, social clubs, and more. Each faction has its own goals, resources, and relationships with others.

## Example Major Factions
- City Council
- Zootopia Police Department (ZPD)
- Zootopia News Network (ZNN)
- Business Guilds (e.g., Market Association, Dockworkers Union)
- Neighborhood Associations (e.g., Savanna Central Council, Tundratown Assembly)
- Conservation League
- Nocturnal Alliance
- Outback Rangers
- Youth Adventure Club

See individual files in this folder for details on each major faction.
