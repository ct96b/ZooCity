# Ficus Grove

Between the Mongoose River and the Emerald Flows, bordered by Pride Ridge (Tundratown, east) and Inari (Downtown, south). Famous for its giant fig trees and peaceful groves.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Giant Fig Trees: Towering trees with wide canopies.
  - Peaceful Glade: A quiet spot for rest and reflection.
  - Grove Market: Local crafts and snacks.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A rare flower has gone missing from the glade.
  - Someone is carving messages into the fig trees.
  - The market is visited by a mysterious traveler.
