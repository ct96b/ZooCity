# Canal District

A neighborhood of waterways, floating homes, and bridges. The Canal District is perfect for animals who love the water. Boats and rafts are the main way to get around, and the air is always fresh and cool.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Floating Market: Shops and food stalls on boats.
  - Otter Slide: A water slide that goes right into the canal!
  - Bridge Plaza: The main meeting spot for friends and neighbors.

- **Common Residents:** Otters, beavers, ducks, turtles, frogs.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A boat goes missing from the floating market.
  - The otter slide is blocked by something mysterious.
  - A bridge is stuck open, splitting the district in two.
