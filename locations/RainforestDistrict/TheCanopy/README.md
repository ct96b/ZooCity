# The Canopy

A cluster of small islands north of Vine Country, across the Mongoose River. Homes and shops are built high in the trees, connected by rope bridges. The Canopy Market is a popular spot for food and crafts.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Canopy Market: Shops and food stalls in the treetops.
  - Rope Bridge Maze: A fun (and tricky) way to get around.
  - Treehouse Library: A quiet place to read and learn.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - The rope bridge maze is tangled after a storm.
  - A new treehouse is being built, but something is missing.
  - Strange noises are coming from the treetops at night.
