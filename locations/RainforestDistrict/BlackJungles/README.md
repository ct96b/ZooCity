# Black Jungles

On the eastern side of the Rainforest District, bordered by The Meadowlands (NE) and Ficus Grove (south). Dense, mysterious, and full of rare plants.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Shadow Glade: A dark, quiet clearing.
  - Rare Plant Nursery: Home to unique species.
  - Misty Falls: A hidden waterfall.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - Strange noises are coming from the shadows at night.
  - A rare plant has gone missing from the nursery.
  - Misty Falls is rumored to be haunted.
