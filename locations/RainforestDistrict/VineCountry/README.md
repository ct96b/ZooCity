# Vine Country

South of the Mongoose River, next to the Otterdams section of Savanna Central. Known for its winding vines, small river crossings, and lively markets. A major road from Downtown crosses Otterdams and enters Vine Country, joining another road from Vole Gardens before crossing the Mongoose River into The Canopy.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Vine Market: Fresh produce and crafts.
  - River Crossings: Small bridges and fords.
  - Festival Green: A popular spot for celebrations.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A festival is interrupted by a sudden rainstorm.
  - Someone is sabotaging the river crossings.
  - A rare fruit goes missing from the market.
