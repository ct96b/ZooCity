# Muddy Swamp

A marshy area south of The Canopy, west of Vine Country, and north of Vole Gardens (Savanna Central). Full of hidden paths, mud pits, and lots of wildlife.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Mud Paths: Winding trails through the swamp.
  - Frog Pond: A favorite spot for amphibians.
  - Hidden Hollow: A secret meeting place.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - Someone is lost in the swamp after dark.
  - The frog pond is drying up mysteriously.
  - A hidden treasure is rumored to be buried in the mud.
