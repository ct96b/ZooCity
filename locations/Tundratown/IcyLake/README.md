# Icy Lake

A frozen lake north of the Climate Wall, popular for skating, ice fishing, and winter festivals. The ice is thick year-round, and the area is surrounded by snowy pines.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Skating Rink
  - Ice Fishing Huts

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A mysterious crack appears in the ice.
  - A winter festival is interrupted by a snowstorm.
