# Chilly Outskirts

The northern edge of Tundratown, between Meadowlands and the Polar Strait. The ice here extends to the edge of Schwarzwald near Grizzly Falls. Known for its cold winds and remote feel.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Windy Bluffs
  - Ice Bridge

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A bridge is blocked by drifting snow.
  - A rare animal is spotted near the bluffs.
