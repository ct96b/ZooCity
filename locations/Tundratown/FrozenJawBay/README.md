# Frozen Jaw Bay

A bay extending west from the Polar Strait, known for its jagged ice formations and chilly winds. Ships and icebreakers dock here, and the bay is a gateway to the east.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Ice Docks
  - Frozen Cliffs

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A ship is trapped in the ice.
  - Strange lights are seen on the cliffs at night.
