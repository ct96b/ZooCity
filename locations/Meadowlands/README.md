# Meadowlands

A wedge between the Rainforest District and Tundratown, on the north edge of the city. Known for its open fields, wildflowers, and peaceful atmosphere.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Meadow Market
  - Wildflower Fields

- **Common Residents:** Sheep, goats, rabbits, and other grazing animals.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A rare animal is spotted in the fields.
  - The market is visited by a mysterious traveler.
