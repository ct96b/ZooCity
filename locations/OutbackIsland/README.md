# Outback Island

Outback Island is a long, narrow island about 3 miles long and 1 mile wide, with its long axis running roughly NNE to SSW. It sits about 5 miles out into Zootopia Sound, separated from the city by open water.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Rocky Cliffs: Steep cliffs on the windward side, popular with nesting birds.
  - Sandy Beaches: Sheltered coves on the leeward side, perfect for swimming and picnics.
  - Outback Station: A small dock and ranger outpost for visitors and researchers.
  - Eucalyptus Grove: A stand of tall trees home to koalas and other wildlife.

- **Common Residents:** Kangaroos, koalas, wallabies, seabirds, lizards, and visiting researchers.

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A rare animal is spotted in the eucalyptus grove.
  - A storm strands visitors on the island overnight.
  - Strange lights are seen from the cliffs at night.
