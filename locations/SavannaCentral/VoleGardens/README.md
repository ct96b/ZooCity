# Vole Gardens

A peaceful, green subdistrict known for its gardens, burrows, and small animal communities. A major road leads north to Vine Country (Rainforest District).

- **Notable Features:**
  - Community Gardens
  - Burrow Market
  - Picnic Fields

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A garden contest is sabotaged.
  - A vole family is searching for a lost heirloom.
