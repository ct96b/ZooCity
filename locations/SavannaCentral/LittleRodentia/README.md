# Little Rodentia

A tiny, bustling neighborhood north of Savanna Plaza, designed for the smallest animals. Full of miniature buildings, vehicles, and parks.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Miniature Park
  - Rodent Raceway
  - Cheese Shop Row

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A runaway racecar causes a commotion.
  - Someone is sneaking cheese from the shops.
  - A big animal accidentally gets stuck in Little Rodentia!
