# Savanna Central

Savanna Central is the lush, open, and diverse southwest district of ZooCity. At its heart is Savanna Plaza, a bustling hub for animals from all over the city. The district is surrounded by vibrant neighborhoods, rivers, and markets, making it a center of activity and culture.

- **Notable Features:**
	- Savanna Plaza: The central gathering place for festivals, markets, and city events.
	- City Hall: Where important decisions are made.
	- Main Train Station: Connects all neighborhoods.
	- Open Markets: Stalls selling food, crafts, and souvenirs from every district.
	- Mole Harbor: A small bay on the NE side of Gnu York, busy with boats and trade.

- **Common Residents:** Lions, elephants, rabbits, foxes, gnus, moles, otters, and animals from all over ZooCity.

## Subdistricts
- Little Rodentia (north of Savanna Plaza)
- Otterdams (north of Little Rodentia)
- Vole Gardens (northwest of the Plaza)
- Haymarket (west of the Plaza, across an unnamed river)
- Gnu York (south of the Plaza, across the same river)
- Mole Harbor (NE side of Gnu York)
- Acorn Heights (across the river from Mole Harbor, adjacent to Downtown and Sahara Square)
- Woody Slopes (north side of Bayou Bay, across from Acorn Heights)
