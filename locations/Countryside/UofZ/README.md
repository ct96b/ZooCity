# University of Zootopia (U of Z) - Main Campus

The University of Zootopia (U of Z) is a prestigious institution located in the countryside outside the city. Known for its sprawling campus, research facilities, and natural beauty, U of Z attracts students from all over the region.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Historic Lecture Halls
  - Agricultural Research Center
  - Wildlife Reserve
  - Countryside Library

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A rare animal is spotted in the wildlife reserve.
  - A research breakthrough draws media attention.
  - A countryside festival brings together students and locals.
