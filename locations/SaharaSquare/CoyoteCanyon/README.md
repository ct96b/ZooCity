# Coyote Canyon

A rugged canyon extending south from the Canyonlands, between Hyenahurst and Sandy Ridge. Known for its winding trails, coyote dens, and adventurous spirit.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Coyote Dens
  - Canyon Trails

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A coyote pup is missing.
  - A race through the canyon is interrupted by a rockslide.
