# Rocky Humps

A rugged area on the southeast coast, bordering Big Dunes and Coyote Canyon, with Sandy Cove to the northeast. Known for its rocky outcrops, tide pools, and dramatic coastline.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Tide Pools
  - Rocky Overlook

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A rare shell is found in the tide pools.
  - A storm washes something unusual ashore.
