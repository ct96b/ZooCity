# Hyenahurst

A lively neighborhood bordering Acorn Heights (Savanna) and Downtown, south of the Canyonlands. Known for its energetic residents, bustling markets, and community events.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Hyena Plaza
  - Night Market

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A prank war gets out of hand.
  - The night market is visited by a mysterious stranger.
