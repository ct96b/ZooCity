# Canyonlands

A dramatic landscape of red rock canyons and mesas, running east-west between Downtown and the Polar Strait, just south of the Climate Wall. Known for echoing winds, hidden caves, and adventurous residents.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Echo Caves
  - Mesa Overlook
  - Canyon Market

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A mysterious echo is heard at night.
  - Someone is lost in the maze of canyons.
