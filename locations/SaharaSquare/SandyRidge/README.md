# Sandy Ridge

A sun-baked ridge stretching from the eastern edge of Canyonlands into the Polar Strait, with Sandy Cove to the south. Known for its sandy slopes, wind-carved rocks, and panoramic views.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Sandy Slopes
  - Wind Rock Arch

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A sandstorm buries part of the ridge.
  - A hidden cave is discovered after a landslide.
