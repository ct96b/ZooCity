# Sahara Square

Sahara Square stretches from the Climate Wall in the north to Zootopia Sound in the south. It is a sun-baked, colorful district full of sand dunes, palm trees, and unique neighborhoods, each with their own character and climate.

- **Notable Features:**
	- Oasis Pool: A cool swimming spot in the middle of the square.
	- Sandcastle Arena: Where animals compete in sand art and building contests.
	- Date Market: Stalls selling sweet treats and cool drinks.
	- Sandy Cove: A sheltered bay on the NE coast, popular for swimming and boating.

- **Common Residents:** Camels, desert foxes, lizards, meerkats, hyenas, coyotes, goats, and more.

## Subdistricts
- Canyonlands (E/W between Downtown and the Polar Strait, south of the Climate Wall)
- Hyenahurst (borders Acorn Heights and Downtown, south of Canyonlands)
- Coyote Canyon (extends south from Canyonlands between Hyenahurst and Sandy Ridge)
- Sandy Ridge (from the eastern edge of Canyonlands into the Polar Strait, with Sandy Cove to the south)
- Big Dunes (south of Hyenahurst, east of Lion's Horn Bay)
- Rocky Humps (SE coast, bordering Big Dunes and Coyote Canyon, Sandy Cove to the NE)
- Sandy Cove (NE coast, south of Sandy Ridge and east of Rocky Humps)

	- A sandstorm uncovers a hidden tunnel.

	- A lost caravan is spotted in the Big Dunes.
	- A mysterious echo is heard in the Canyonlands at night.
