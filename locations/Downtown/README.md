# Downtown (Central Outcrop)

Downtown is the vibrant city center of ZooCity, sitting atop the massive rocky outcrop at the heart of the island. It is the hub for business, government, and citywide events.

- **Notable Features:**
	- Festival Square
	- Main Library
	- City Hall
	- Mongoose River Walk

- **Common Residents:** Animals from all over ZooCity, especially those working in government, business, and the arts.
