# Zootopia Tech (ZT) - Administration

Zootopia Tech (ZT) is the city’s most academically rigorous institution, focusing on graduate and postgraduate education, research, and innovation. The main administration building is located in Downtown, but ZT’s research facilities are small, specialized, and spread throughout ZooCity and the surrounding region.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Graduate Studies Office
  - Research Grant Center
  - Faculty Lounge

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A breakthrough experiment needs urgent help.
  - Rival schools compete in a citywide science fair.
