# University of Zootopia (U of Z) - Downtown Satellite Campus

U of Z maintains a satellite campus in Downtown ZooCity, providing urban students with access to select programs, classes, and research opportunities.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Urban Research Center
  - Downtown Lecture Hall
  - Student Lounge

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A visiting professor from the countryside campus goes missing.
  - A citywide academic competition is hosted here.
