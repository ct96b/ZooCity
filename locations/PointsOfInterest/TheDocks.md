# The Docks

Located along the southwest edge of Haymarket and the western edge of Gnu York, The Docks are a bustling hub for trade, fishing, and travel. Ferries and cargo ships come and go at all hours.

- **Notable Features:**
  - Ferry Terminals
  - Fish Market

- **Adventure Ideas:**
  - A shipment goes missing overnight.
  - A stowaway is discovered on a ferry.
