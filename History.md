# History of Zootopia: Major World Events

This document provides a historical overview of Zootopia's world, focusing on major events that parallel real-world US history. These events are given proxy names and details, allowing Game Masters to reference or adapt them as needed for educational or narrative purposes.

---

## The Great Herd War (World War II)
**Era:** Great-Grandparents' Generation  
A devastating global conflict that reshaped the world order. The Great Herd War saw alliances of predator and prey nations clash over territory, ideology, and resources. The aftermath led to the founding of the United Mammal Nations (UMN), dedicated to peace and cooperation.

## The Frosted Curtain Standoff (The Cold War)
**Era:** Grandparents' Generation  
A prolonged period of tension between the Northern Frostlands Bloc and the Southern Sunlands Pact. The Frosted Curtain Standoff was marked by espionage, proxy skirmishes, and an arms race, but never escalated into open war. The era ended with the "Thawing Accords," easing global tensions.

## The Twin Tusk Tragedy & The Global War on Tusk (9/11 & GWOT)
**Era:** Parents' Generation  
A shocking attack on the Twin Tusk Towers in Gnu York led to a worldwide campaign against extremist factions. The Global War on Tusk changed security, politics, and society. Gnu York now hosts the "Twin Tusk Memorial Park" to honor those lost and reflect on the event's impact.

## The Great Sneeze (COVID-19 Pandemic)
**Era:** Present  
A mysterious and highly contagious illness swept across the globe, affecting all species and communities. The Great Sneeze led to lockdowns, new health protocols, and lasting social changes. The world is still adapting to its aftereffects, with ongoing debates about public health and community resilience.

## The Night Howler Crisis (Events of the Film)
**Era:** Present  
An unprecedented wave of predator attacks gripped Zootopia, driven by a mysterious substance known as Night Howler. The crisis tested the city's unity and exposed deep-seated prejudices, but ultimately led to greater understanding and reforms in law enforcement and interspecies relations. The Night Howler Crisis is a recent memory, shaping current attitudes and policies across the city.

---

These proxy events provide a framework for exploring Zootopia's recent history, offering opportunities for learning, reflection, and storytelling within the game setting.
